# PongKdor.com Project Plan

**Project Name**: PongKdor.com - Meme Men's Lifestyle with Humor  
**Tagline**: "Where Cambodian Bros Keep It Real... and Weird"  
**Core Concept**: A humorous men's lifestyle website celebrating "Pong Kdor" (Khmer slang) energy through memes, relatable stories, fitness, confidence, and unhinged bro humor. Focus on clothing/merch and content.

## 1. Vision & Concept

PongKdor.com will become the go-to destination for young Cambodian men who want laughs, real talk, and motivation wrapped in local slang humor. 

**Core Pillars**:
- **Humor First**: Self-deprecating, absurd, dark-ish but fun Khmer bro energy.
- **Relatable Lifestyle**: Fitness, dating fails, grooming, money, mom pressure, Phnom Penh life.
- **Weird & Deep**: Push boundaries with weird series while staying cartoon/meme only.
- **Merch**: Meme clothing and stickers as natural extension.

**Target Audience**: Cambodian guys 18-35 in Phnom Penh + diaspora.

**Monetization**:
- Merch sales (Print-on-Demand)
- Affiliate links (supplements, grooming)
- Ads (once traffic grows)
- Future: Premium funny content or physical drops

## 2. Content Style & Examples

**Tone**: Playful, unfiltered, bro-to-bro, heavy slang mix (Khmer + English).

### Example Article Titles & Descriptions

1. **"My Pong Kdor Betrayed Me During Khmer New Year"**  
   Story + memes about embarrassing moments.

2. **"Small Pong Kdor Club: Survival Guide"**  
   Empowerment through savage self-roast.

3. **"Pong Kdor MythBusters: Does Beer Really Help?"**  
   Humorous "tests" with cartoon illustrations.

4. **"How to Bless Your Pong Kdor at the Temple (Without Mom Knowing)"**  
   Absurd spiritual humor.

5. **Tier List: Cambodian Motos Ranked by Vibration Strength**  
   Peak weird content.

**Visual Style**: Cartoon characters, bold red/black/gold colors, Khmer-inspired fonts mixed with meme style.

## 3. Compliance & Safety Guidelines

**Main Rules to Avoid Issues**:
- All visuals = cartoons, drawings, text memes ONLY. No real nudes or explicit photos.
- Frame everything as "humor & lifestyle".
- Add 18+ age gate on homepage.
- No selling or promoting illegal services/products.
- Moderate all user submissions.
- Disclaimer: "All jokes bro, keep it fun and consensual."
- Monitor Cambodian laws on online content — stay away from hardcore obscenity.

**Risk Level**: Low-Medium if we follow cartoon + humor rule.

## 4. MVP (Minimum Viable Product)

**MVP Goal**: Launch a basic working site in 4-6 weeks.

**MVP Features**:
- Homepage with hero banner + latest posts
- Blog with 8-10 initial articles
- Simple Shopify store with 6-8 products (T-shirts + sticker packs)
- About page + disclaimer
- Mobile-friendly design
- Basic analytics (Google Analytics)

**Tech Stack**:
- Shopify for store
- WordPress or Carrd/Framer for blog (or integrate into Shopify)
- Canva for designs

## 5. Timeline Planning (From Now to Infinity)

### Phase 1: Foundation (Week 1-4)
- Finalize branding, logo, taglines
- Create 10 articles + 8 product designs
- Set up Shopify + domain
- Build MVP site
- **Launch soft** (friends & small social push)

### Phase 2: Early Growth (Month 2-3)

- Run small Facebook/TikTok ads

### Phase 3: Weird Era (Month 4-6)
- Launch weird series (Confessions, MythBusters, Tier Lists)

- Drop limited merch collections
- Aim for consistent traffic

### Phase 4: Scaling (Month 6-12)
- Add more merch (hoodies, custom orders)
- YouTube/TikTok video version of content
- Affiliate partnerships
- Possible physical pop-up in PP

### Phase 5: Legacy (Year 2+)
- Build community (Discord or moderated forum)
- Annual "Pong Kdor Awards" meme event
- Expand to books/ebooks
- Become the cultural reference for Cambodian guy humor

**End of Time Vision**: PongKdor becomes a recognized Cambodian brand — merch everywhere, content quoted in daily life, maybe even collabs with local artists.
